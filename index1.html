How to play

Tap (or drag) your White King (♔) and other pieces to move them by standard chess rules — reach the top row to clear the floor, and Black replies automatically after every move. Pawns you capture don't just vanish: each one you're still holding when a surviving piece carries over to the next floor comes back as an extra White pawn.
