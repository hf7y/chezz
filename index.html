How to play

You control a lone White King (♔) trying to fight up through an endless dungeon. Each floor is seeded with a random black army — bigger and nastier the deeper you go — scattered across the board above the bottom two ranks.

Click (or tap) a piece to select it, then click a destination to move it — or just drag a piece straight to its destination instead. Clicking a different one of your own pieces mid-selection switches your selection instead of wasting a click, and pressing Escape (or right-clicking the board) cancels a selection outright. Pieces move using standard chess rules, except once a floor's black army is completely wiped out your King can leap to any empty square instead of creeping one step at a time. After you move, the black side replies automatically.

Capture black pieces to bank them (shown above the board). Banked material lets you promote a White pawn into a Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight once that pawn reaches the second-to-last rank.

Get your King to the very top row to clear the floor. Surviving pieces carry over to the next floor's back ranks, surviving pawns advance one rank, and any non-pawn survivor gets escorted by a bonus pawn if you've banked a captured black pawn to spend. Rank numbers on the left keep climbing across floors so you can track your overall depth.

Your progress is saved in the URL, so the page is reload- and share-safe, and the browser back button undoes moves. Use "Reset" below the board to start over from floor 1.
